Variant calling¶

Best practice pipeline¶

Briefly describe best practice pipeline (e.g. GATK, bcftools, freebayes), highlighting some of the problems with natural populations:

  • many workflows are human-centric and don’t scale to non-model organisms (e.g. chromosome numbers in GATK)
